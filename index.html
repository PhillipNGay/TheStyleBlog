About style.

People usually think that in order for a man to dress himself stylishly, that he must have plenty of money and/or clothes at his disposal. In reality, nothing could be further from the truth. Yes, that might be true in order to keep up with the latest innovations of high fashion. However, there is a difference between fashion and style. Dressing stylishly is not about attempting to appear as certain designers would have you appear. It is about accentuating who you already are, presenting yourself in the manner most flattering to your form, and perhaps even your personality. It's a very skillful balance between form and function. Such balance is attainable for the affluent and the thrift alike, for both can learn to recognize items of quality and care for them. This blog is written to discuss and celebrate the classic styles of menswear that have endured past trends of the moment!
